Privacy Policy

Auto Clone is a Trello Power-Up that helps users automate recurring card cloning workflows.

Information We Access

The Power-Up may access Trello board data such as cards, lists, labels, and member information required for cloning and scheduling functionality.

How Data Is Used

Data is only used to:

  • Create cloned Trello cards
  • Manage recurring automation schedules
  • Provide Power-Up functionality inside Trello

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data and API access tokens.
